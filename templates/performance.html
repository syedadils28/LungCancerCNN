🏁 Verdict — Which Model to Trust?

🧠 CNN — Primary Diagnostic Model ({{ metrics.cnn.accuracy }}%)

Trained end-to-end on CT scan images, it learns spatial patterns invisible to other models. Treat this as the primary result.

🌲 Random Forest — Validation Model ({{ metrics.rf.accuracy }}%)

Trained on the CNN's own 256-dim features. When RF and CNN agree, confidence is high; when they disagree, flag for review.

📍 KNN — Secondary Validation ({{ metrics.knn.accuracy }}%)

A useful second opinion, typically trailing RF. Use agreement as a confidence booster, not a standalone decision tool.
